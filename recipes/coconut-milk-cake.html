Coconut Milk Cake

Description

Super moist coconut box cake with just a hint of coconut flavor. Frost with your favorite frosting or leave plain.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package white cake mi
  • ¾ cup coconut milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Mix cake mix, coconut milk, eggs, oil, and water together in a bowl using an electric mixer for 2 minutes; pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
